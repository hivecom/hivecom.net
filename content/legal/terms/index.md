### 3.4 Profile Visibility and Forum Content

By default, your profile is only visible to signed-in users. You may choose to make your profile public, which makes it accessible to anyone on the internet, including visitors who are not logged in.

All forum posts and comments are publicly visible. However, for visitors who are not signed in, user identities are anonymized - your username is replaced with a randomly generated pseudonym that stays consistent within a conversation. If your profile is set to public, your real username and profile information are visible alongside your posts to everyone. If your profile is not public, only signed-in users can see your real identity in forum threads.

While anonymization provides a basic layer of protection against automated scraping, it is not a security guarantee. Any signed-in user can see your username and profile information in forum threads regardless of your profile visibility setting.
